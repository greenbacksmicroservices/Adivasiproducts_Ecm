FAQ

Questions sellers ask first

Is seller registration free?

Yes, submitting a seller account request is free. Your account is reviewed before dashboard access is activated.

How can I upload products?

After admin approval, you can use the seller dashboard to add product details, prices, images, and inventory.

When will I receive payment?

Payments are tracked in the seller panel and released through the payout process after orders are verified.

Can I manage orders from mobile?

Yes, the seller experience is responsive, so orders and products can be reviewed from mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Do I need admin approval?

Yes. New seller accounts remain pending until the admin reviews and approves the request.
